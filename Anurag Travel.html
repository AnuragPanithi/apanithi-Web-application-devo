My Travel Blog

I love to Travel from one place to another because I get chance to learn new things and new cultures around me. I like to explore beautiful landscapes take some amazing pictures and I am also into adventure sports. Below are the few best places that I have visited come please have a look.

Manali

Manali is located at the northen part of India and is also a high altitude himalayan town with 6726 feet elevation. This acts as gateway for many beautiful valleys of Himalayan rages, we can do Paragliding, Rafting, skiing and many more adventures here

Kerala

Kerala is known as Gods Own Country. It is in the tropical malabar cost of India with largest shore line of 600KMs and Western Ghats which support the cultivation of Coffee, Tea and various Spice cultivations.

Chikmagalore

Chikmagalore is a hill station in India situated in Karnataka state it trails through many grasslands and peaks. Bhadra wildlife sanctuary here is the ome to many Elephants, Tigers and Leopards
